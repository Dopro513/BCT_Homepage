Use of the Blockchain Terminal is governed by its License Agreement. App prices are set by each app developer and range from freemium apps, one-time purchase, monthly recurring, to pay-per-use. Terminal users may choose which apps they are interested in using based on the price/value of each app, however, users must maintain a minimum balance of BCT in order to login to their account. The minimum BCT balance was set by the BCT Inc on November 1, 2018 at 3,000 BCT. The Minimum BCT balance is a function of the number of apps on Blockchain Terminal, number of users, and other variables; and it is capped at 100,000 BCT. The Blockchain Terminal and BCT are two inseparable components of a single product (like a Jukebox and Jukebox tokens which are required to play songs). BCT is used in a similar manner to Binance’s BNB (users currently receive a 50% discount on trading fees, custodian fees and apps when paying with BCT). The price of BCT is currently fixed at $0.10, however A one-time-discount is offered at the time a terminal is purchased. Like the terminal, the purchase of BCT is covered by our no-questions-asked, 30-day money back guarantee. For example: a $10,000 purchase of 100,000 BCT+40,000 Bonus BCT entitles the purchaser to return 140,000 BCT for refund of $10,000. A partial return of 70,000 BCT entitles the purchaser to a refund of $5,000. Users may request their refund in USD, BTC, or ETH.
